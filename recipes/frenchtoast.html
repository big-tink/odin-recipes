French Toast

A Breakfast Classic

    Ingredients
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/3 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • Sliced Bread - pick your favorite kind
  • Butter - for greasing pan
      • Cooking Instructions
  • Step 1: Preheat pan to low-medium heat.
  • Step 2: Combine eggs, sugar, milk and vanilla in bowl and beat thoroughly.
  • Step 3: Grease heated pan with butter. Enough to cover pan.
  • Step 4: Dip bread in mixture from step 2 and make sure both sides of bread are coated.
  • Step 5: Place coated bread in pan and cook for 2 - 3 minutes or until bottom is golden brown.
  • Step 6: Flip bread and repeat step 5 for cooking the second side.
  • Step 7: Remove from pan and coat with choice of toppings. I prefer butter, syrup and a mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
